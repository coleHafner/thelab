My Goals

Physical Health
  • Run 3 times per week. M-W-F (mornings)
  • Eat smaller portions at meals
  • Reduce candy intake to only 3 pieces per day
  • Eat less fat and more protein
Mental Health
  • Make a great effort to think more positively
  • Watch less TV, read a book instead (only .5 hrs TV per day, MAX)
  • Increase attention span, really try to pay attention when spoken to
    • Only use phone for non-work-related things during break
    • Do not turn back to keyboard until conversation is done
    • Truly try to understand instead of just auto-accepting. THINK CRITICALLY!
    • Ask questions if you do not understand, but only after giving it some thought
  • DO NOT watch porn.
    • It dulls the mind
    • There are so many other things in this world that deserve my attention before porn. Anything, really
    • It takes over my thought stream
  • Read books more often. This will help to develop my mind and increase my attention span
  • Take yourself seriously!
  • Respect yourself
Career
  • Be at least 10 mins early per day
  • Wear clothes that fit well and look sharp.
    • Buy new clothes (white shirts + collared non-button-up shirts. Solid, dark color)
    • Iron shirts possibly
  • Be professional and forthcoming in interactions with co-workers. If you don't know something, say so.
  • Possibly do hair?
  • Listen to your coworkers ideas and seriously think about them. Communicate with them when you are stuck.
